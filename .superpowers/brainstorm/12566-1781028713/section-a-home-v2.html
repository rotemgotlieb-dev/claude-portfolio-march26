FIX 1Hover-arrow no longer overlaps the title. Friction cards now reserve padding-right: 36px so the title and meta text wrap inside the card's content area, leaving the arrow its own zone. The arrow also sits at opacity: 0.35 when idle (a hint of clickability) and animates to full opacity + slides 2px left on hover. Standing rule added to pulse/learnings.md.

FIX 2Canvas filled with realistic Kanban content. Four columns with real task names sourced from the actual product's mock dataset (Refactor authentication flow, Update onboarding copy, Fix mobile nav overflow, etc.) plus severity dots, due dates, and assignee avatars. The heatmap blob blooms over the "Settings panel hierarchy / Toggle label fix" zone. Mock content cost is minimal; review-quality cost of blank mocks is huge.

FIX 3First-visit tour overlay added. Confirmed product feature. 4 steps total: (1) Today's priority strip, (2) page tabs with severity signal, (3) layer toggle with distinct lenses, (4) friction zones + what-changed panel. Dismissible per step, persisted to localStorage, never re-shows once skipped or completed. Standard product-tour pattern (Linear, Stripe, Figma onboarding reference).

Theme toggle now visible in the left rail bottom (moon icon). Dark mode ships in Phase 1.
