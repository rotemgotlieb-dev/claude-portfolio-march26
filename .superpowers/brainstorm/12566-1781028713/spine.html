Why I recommend A

Three loops match Ghost's saturation ceiling and keep the page rhythmic without forcing motion. Each loop dramatizes a real decision (Solution: "I built one canvas that fuses time+signal+component," Pivot: "I scrapped V1 because conventional has a ceiling," Process: "I shipped the Generate Fix loop you can actually click"). The AE loop drops because it argues HOW you built rather than WHAT Pulse is, and the prototype-as-the-center direction makes WHAT Pulse is the load-bearing question. Live link CTA in Outcome lets curious viewers go deeper; the case study itself stays tight.
