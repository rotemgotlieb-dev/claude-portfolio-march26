Overview

I design the page, then ship the last mile myself in the repo.

Freelance design engineering for Surface Labs, a public YC company building an AI-native GTM conversion platform. I designed marketing pages in Claude, built them in the production repo with Claude Code, and shipped them as reviewed pull requests.

Designed in Claude, shipped in code, merged to production. The cursor is the designer. It types the data, the page builds itself.

The System

On-brand by construction.

A new page is a typed data object plus a three-line route. Nothing else. Content lives in a typed data file, layout lives in a template, and the two never mix.

One typed data object. One template. A finished, on-brand page.
