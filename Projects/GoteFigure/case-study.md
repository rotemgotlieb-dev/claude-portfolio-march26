# GoteFigure -- Hand-Drawn Apparel Brand

## Metadata

- **Context:** Personal Brand
- **Role:** Founder, Illustrator, Brand Designer
- **Timeline:** 2020 -- Present
- **Tools:** Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Wacom, Shopify

---

## Overview

### Every design starts as a hand drawing.

GoteFigure is my illustrated apparel brand. Drawn on a Wacom, vectorized in Illustrator, then built into a full brand identity and Shopify storefront. 200+ items sold in the first quarter.

---

## Brand System

### From sketch to listed product in under 3 hours.

A lightweight design system -- type scale, color palette, social templates, and product mockup workflows -- that made the whole process repeatable and fast.

---

## Storefront

### Designed for how people actually shop on mobile.

I designed the Shopify experience -- product page layout, checkout flow, and mobile UX. Managed all photography, listing copy, and inventory.

---

## Outcome

### Impact

The store is currently offline as I prepare a relaunch with updated branding and new product lines. This project taught me how design systems thinking applies far outside product design.
